How the hCASH LP Moat Works
⚠ EEL Penalty
If you exit a lock early, the EEL (Early Exit Liquidity) penalty applies: from 95% penalty at start to 0% when fully matured. Penalty tokens go to the protocol + other stakers.
🏰 Fort Score
Your Fort Score = all your Moat Points summed across all moats. Fort Score % of total ecosystem = your share of the Early User Airdrop.
💧 LP vs Token Moat
Unlike regular token moats, hCASH Moat accepts LP tokens (not raw hCASH). This means you provide liquidity AND earn points — a dual reward for supporting the hCASH/AVAX trading pair.
